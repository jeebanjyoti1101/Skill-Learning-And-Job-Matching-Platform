Model Details
Vector Search: TF-IDF + Cosine Similarity builds semantic relevance between your skills and job skill vectors.
Classification: Random Forest estimates match probability from skill overlap, related-skill ratio, experience fit, and salary alignment.
Ranking: XGBoost and Gradient Boosting score relevance; UI surfaces the stronger rank signal per job.
Hybrid Score: final ranking combines cosine, RF probability, and ranker output with weighted aggregation.
Validation Focus: primary model quality uses stricter family-holdout metrics to reduce optimistic overfit readings.
